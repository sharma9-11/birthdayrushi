For You,

Some people walk into your life quietly,
and somehow become the loudest part of your happiness.
You didn’t just become my best friend,
you became the place I go when the world feels too heavy.
I don’t think you realize how much light you carry,
how effortlessly you make things better just by being there.
If I could, I’d pause time on the moments we laugh the hardest,
just so they never have to end.
You’re not just important to me,
you’re irreplaceable in ways I’ll probably never fully explain.
And on your birthday,
I don’t just celebrate the day you were born.
I celebrate the fact that somehow,
out of everyone in this world…
I got lucky enough to know you.

- Sajal
